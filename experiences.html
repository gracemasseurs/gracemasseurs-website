STORIES

Here, multiple Outward Bound alumni give you a taster of what their courses were like for them. The experiences, the friendships and the memories.


MIND BODY SOUL

The actual day to day activities that you will take part in during your time at Outward Bound are unique to every course. However, waking up in the early morning for some fitness is a classic.


ANAKIWA

21 day Course. For those willing to step outside their comfort zone. Outward Bound offers the unique opportunity find something in yourself that you never knew was there.


FOOD

The food at Outward Bound is delicious! Prepared by the amazing chef Marcel, attendees are treated to the best of the best. Your taste buds will be sure to change when your tired and hungry as well - any type of food will do!


LEADERSHIP COURSE

Attending Outward Bound is a sure way to make friends for life. Spending every day with each other for 3 weeks can 14 strangers closer than ever. Singing, dancing sharing in each others pain when running 21km!


NATURE

Anakiwa is simply stunning. Throughout the three weeks you will experience all the wonders of this magical place by boat and by foot.
